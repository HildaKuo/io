Urban Clean

Background
In the small space, when users pour trash into a trash bin by using a dustpan, the trash always fall on the ground easily. Besides, the Taipei city residents are required to purchase specially designated garbage bags for waste disposal. Therefore, I want to design a new trash bin not only fix the issue but also save using the paid trash bags.

Understand User Needs
The garbage can should fit paid trash bag. ($$)
The garbage can provide the function to compress garbage.($$)
The garbage can should be touchless.
The garbage should be provide hands-free operation to keep germs at bay
The garbage can should with a lid to keep bugs away
The garbage can should with a lid to eliminating the odor leakage
The garbage can should prevent the bag from falling into the can
The garbage can should be easy and quick to throw trash
The garbage can should fit ergonomics
The garbage can should prevent the trash fall out of the garbage can

 

Decomposition by user needs


Concepts develop


 

Concept selection

Prototypes
Increase the side grooves, which dustpan can be jammed to prevent trash slipping.

When the cover closed, it compasses the trash automatically.

A switch to open holes, it is easy to clean the trash can and water can flow easily.

The dustpan with narrow side help to pour trash easily.
Change the handle to the side, which is more ergonomic and add a button on the stick to open the lid.

 

Concept Testing

Target user : urban housewife 
Location : supermarket
Age : 25~45
Number : 10 people
